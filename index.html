The Gospel

GOSPEL – A Simple Outline

G – God
God loves you.

O – Our Sins
Our sins separate us from God.

S – Savior
Jesus is the only way.

P – Pay
Jesus paid the penalty for sin.

E – Eternal Life
God offers it freely.

L – Live
Live for Him.

Simple. Hopeful. Life-changing.
