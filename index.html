The Gospel

GOSPEL – A Simple Outline
○○○○○○

G – God
God loves you.

God’s love is personal and intentional. He knows you and cares deeply about your life.

O – Our Sins
Our sins separate us from God.

Sin breaks our relationship with God, but separation is not the end of the story.

S – Savior
Jesus is the only way.

Jesus bridges the gap between God and humanity through His life, death, and resurrection.

P – Pay
Jesus paid the penalty for sin.

His sacrifice means forgiveness is a gift, not something we earn.

E – Eternal Life
God offers it freely.

Eternal life begins now through a relationship with Jesus.

L – Live
Live for Him.

Following Jesus is a daily journey of trust, growth, and love.
Simple. Hopeful. Life-changing.
